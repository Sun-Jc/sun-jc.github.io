About Me

Hi, my name is Jingchang. Now I am a graduate student at Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Science (IIIS), Tsinghua University. In 2016, I joined Network Science Group of IIIS, with Prof. Thomas Moscibroda as my adviser. Then in 2019, I joined Computational Economics Group of IIIS, with Prof, Pingzhong Tang as my adviser. My research interests include blockchain, network science, and machine learning.
